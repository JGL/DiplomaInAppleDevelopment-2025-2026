Which anchors are available? Have a look at the ARKit in visionOS homepage for a complete list. TLDR: you must choose one of:
  • Hand anchors (hands will be the interaction method for all apps, but you want to augment hands specifically)
  • Plane anchors or World anchors - building an experience that works with any floor, table or wall (pick vertical or horizontal) - being able to return to the same space and see the same virtual objects
  • Image anchors - tracking flat images
  • Object anchors - tracking 3D objects
Do any stand out to anyone already? Let's discuss. I recommend picking either Plane or Hand anchors as multiple Image anchors are not properly supported by Reality Composer Pro and Object anchors require a large amount of processing power to train.
How the anchor relates to your BA or wider practice. If you can't decide, then go for hands or planes? Let's discuss.
